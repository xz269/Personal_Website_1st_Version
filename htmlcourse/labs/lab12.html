Earlier this week (on July 20), a crew of astronauts and engineers started their space mission ... underwater. It's the start of NEEMO 20 (NASA Extreme Environment Mission Operations) where the team dove to a base that is 62 feet (19 meters) below the surface off the coast of Florida, giving them little more than a trailer-sized living space to live in.
