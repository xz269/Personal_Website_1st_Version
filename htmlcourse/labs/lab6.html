Big Hero 6

Big Hero 6 is one of the very popular American films which released in 2014. The film is about the story of a young genius name Hiro who forms a superhero team to fight with a masked villain. A character called Baymax raises a hot robot trend all round the world because of the film. Its adorable looking and characteristic warms everyone.

The film features the voices of Scott Adsit, Ryan Potter, Daniel Henney, T. J. Miller, Jamie Chung, Damon Wayans, Jr., Génesis Rodríguez, Alan Tudyk, James Cromwell, and Maya Rudolph.
