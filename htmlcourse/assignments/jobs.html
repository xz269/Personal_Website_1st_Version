Independent Wedding Planner
Join our team of award-winning planners and gain new clients through our planning tool.


Responsibilities

+ Pass our planner skills assessment tests with a score of 85% or higher.
+ Maintain a customer satisfaction rating of 90% or higher.
+ Provide relevant and useful advice on all aspects of a wedding.
+ Contribute to our local vendor database with reviews only planners can see.
+ Up-to-date with the latest wedding trends.
+ Timely response to booking requests.
+ Agree to offer 100% customer satisfaction.
+ Maintain acceptable liability insurance.
+ Be a mentor to your junior wedding planners.


What we offer

+ New clients through our planning tool.
+ A team of junior planners assigned to help you with onsite coordination.
+ Weekly training workshops held virtually.
+ Photograph rights to build your portfolio.


Qualifications

+ 2-5 years of experience in wedding planning.
+ Comfortable with the latest technology, including video conferencing.
+ A demonstrated passion for weddings.
+ Knowledgable about budget, design, and logistics management.
+ Calm and resourceful under pressured situations.
+ Keen ability to adapt to change.


Compensation and perks

+ Up to $40/hour, depending on experience.
+ Paid weekly upon completion of work.
+ Invitations to exclusive events.
+ Work remotely.

Required experience:

Planning: 2 years
